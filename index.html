BKM Data Fellowship Portfolio of Work

Riain Fitzsimons
Brooklyn Museum Data Science Fellow, MS Data Analytics and Visualization

This project used an imbalanced classification model to predict which museum visitors were most likely to respond to a targeted membership campaign. The model was trained on historical engagement, visitation patterns, ticketing behavior, and proximity to the museum.

Feature importance, calibration, and lift testing were used to refine outputs, allowing the museum to target the top 10% of visitors with the highest predicted probability of conversion. The final model significantly outperformed random mailing and is being considered for production integration.
